Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at Markifly.

Introduction

At Markifly, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect data when you visit our website (markifly.in) or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal and non-personal information, including:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and company details (via contact forms).
  • Technical data such as browser type, IP address, and cookies for analytics purposes.
  • Any data voluntarily shared when communicating with our team.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide requested services.
  • Improve our website and enhance user experience.
  • Send updates, newsletters, and marketing communications (with consent).
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance functionality and analyze site traffic. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement strong technical and organizational measures to secure your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online transmission is completely secure, and we encourage responsible data sharing.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools (like Google Analytics, Email Services, or Social Media Plugins) that collect limited user data under their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. To make a request, please contact us at info@markifly.in.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. The latest version will always be available on this page.
